About Us

FinMitra is more than just a financial app; we're building a financially empowered community in rural India. We understand the unique challenges faced by individuals in these communities and are committed to providing accessible financial education and support in local languages. Our AI-powered platform offers personalized learning, tailored micro-investment opportunities, smart budgeting tools, and a secure banking interface. We also connect users with mentors and entrepreneurs, fostering peer-to-peer learning and driving positive change in local economies.
